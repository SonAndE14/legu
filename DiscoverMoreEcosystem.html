Rearmed
TheShark ┃ CEO September 16, 2021 The Rearmed project Begins

Many of us are given the idea and a full vision of what we need, be it money, fame or just a little fun moment. We all come up with good ideas, we just have to put them into practice. Rearmed was born as a simple brainstorming between a couple of developers, who had different experiences in different fields of the great world of Cryptocurrencies. They stopped the afternoon discussing different ideas, to form a project, to which one of them exclaimed Why not do it from NFT? Non-fungible tokens are very popular these days, I know a network called Atomic Hub where we can get started.

The idea was wonderful, but there were still doubts, to which a member of the team replied, How can we call you? There are many names and almost all of them are Used! they chatted and chatted, thousands of ideas passed through their heads, but without a name to place it, the idea would be discarded like the previous ones ... Normally that happens, said the CMO without any obcion. Rearmed sounds a good name in his words, expressed the team's COO. director General exclaimed with doubt and surprised at such a name. What is its meaning? The COO answered firmly, we have all had good ideas, most of them discarded for simple and oddities that does not make sense, why not Make it fun and put all the ideas together. That's what Rearmed means!

With great joy the project was launched, now Rearmed is known as an NFT project that works under the Atomic Hub ┃ WAX network, its main theme is monsters, it is a proactive community that advances every day. Currently Rearmed resives monthly updates and it is expected that the project will soon be known as by many people to lago plaso. Not all monsters are scary can be cute and adorable, besides that an imagination Creative Makes a Difference.

Rearmed was officially released to the public on September 16, 2021.
