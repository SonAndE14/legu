This is a competition that is run every season fairly. Before starting the competition, there should be just a brief explanation, remembering that everyone is the same when participating and must follow the rules or they will be automatically disqualified. A brief explanation of bestial growth.

What is Bestial Growth? It is an event that takes place per season, where participants must invite their friends, giving them a brief introduction of what Rearmed is, how it works and what is the vision of said project. Each of the participants must follow the following parameters if they want to participate: 1) You must invite a person, friend or acquaintance giving a short introduction of what the project is. 2) You must place a statement or post on Twitter where you refer to the Rearmed project by tagging the communities referring to NFTs. 3) You must make a constructive criticism in a Social Network of your preference about Rearmed NFT. 4) The invited person must identify himself and name the person who mentioned the project, showing a proof or capture of what was done. 5) The winner will be the one who invites more friends.

These rules will not be changed once the game has started, however it should be mentioned that if any of these are broken the player will be disqualified from the competition. We are very strict with the rules. SPAM will NOT be taken into account since it is an unpleasant and unprofessional act that will also give a bad image of the project. That is why we recommend that it be only with your friends or acquaintances.

What is the Purpose of holding this event? It is very simple, it is about making the community grow in a healthy way, that is why this competition does not take into account SPAM or MULTI-ACCOUNTS since multi-accounts will be banned instantly and SPAM is an unpleasant and unprofessional act at the moment of referencing a project. These events usually have a predetermined time to start and end.
